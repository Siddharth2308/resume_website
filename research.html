Automotive Grade Dead Reconking | Brose

2023- Under Process for Publication

Research Paper on low cost 3 Dimensional Dead Reckoning System developed with Brose India, that will assist the gps and provide navigational support in absence of GPS signal or when the signal is weak. We used the IMU of GY5-21, ADXL-345 etc. for the Implementation. Click on the title to read project report.

Research & Development on Robotics Drive Systems

2023- Finalizing

R&D on 3 wheel omni-holonomic drive, 4 wheel omni-holonomic drive, 3 wheel swere drive. The focus of the research was to develop a standard system for implementing highly precise ominidirectional on these drives. Making the system user friendly and easy to use for any non technical person. Studied and implemented various types of feedback for the same.
